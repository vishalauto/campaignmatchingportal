SSDI Script

Hi, this is ___ calling you from the Subsidy Department.
Well, how are you doing today?

The reason for my call is just to let you know that your name and number have been selected to receive some free subsidy money where you can qualify for $500 to $1200 each, and along with additional benefits which you never have to pay back.

You also don’t need to provide any of your personal or banking information. This is a totally free scheme coming through the Government, and you don’t have to pay a single dollar out of pocket.

✅ Benefits Overview

In this call, you will get 2 benefits from the government:

  1. Self-Development Benefit – You will receive government benefits and lots of rewards.
  2. Overage Benefits – Like grocery support, free health insurance, and gift cards for referrals.

And again – you never have to pay anything.

📝 Qualification Questions

Now, may I know your age (45–63)?

Okay! So let me tell you why your name and number has been selected: Due to recession, Govt used to make a survey of those people who pay their bills and taxes time to time, and from every state, 50 numbers get selected — and your number is one of them. That’s why you’re selected, okay?

So, this time, all these benefits are coming from the Disability Department, which is why you will need to speak with my Disability Advisor. They will ask you some simple qualifying questions.

🖊️ Preparation

Now is that possible — can you grab a pen and piece of paper so you can write down every question with the answer?

If customer says “Hold on, let me grab it”
➡️ Okay, you can take your time and let me know when you are ready. Alright?

✅ Main Questions

Q1: Do you Expect to Be Out of Work At least 1 Year Due To your Health Condition?

✅ Say: “Yes, then I can qualify for Disability.”

If Customer is Working, tell him/her:
"They are just asking a question and they are not going to check if you are working or not. So for now, tell them ‘No, I am not going to work for the next 1 year.’ Okay?"

Q2: How Many Times Were You Treated by a Doctor, Hospital or Clinic in the Last Year?

✅ Say: “More than 2 times.” Alright?

Q3: Have You Had a Full-Time Job Working At Least 30 Hours Per Week in the Past 5 Years?

✅ Say: “Yes, I had a job in the past where I was working more than 30 hours per week.” Alright?

Q4: Have You Worked a Total of 5 Years in The Last 10 Years Where Your Social Security Tax Was Deducted?

✅ Say: “Yes, I was working 5 years in the last 10 years, and my Social Security was deducted from there.”

Q5: Are You Taking Any Medicine or Active Prescription from a Doctor?

✅ Say: “Yes, I’m taking some medicine given by my doctor.”

📌 Also remind customer to say this when asked:

“I am not working and I have no lawyer or advocate with me.”

(Because they might ask you this — please note this well also.)

🩺 Medical Condition

What is your medical condition? Like, I hope you are fit and healthy. But if not, please let me know so I can apply for additional benefits also.

If you don’t have a condition, you can mention one of these:
✅ Heart Disease, Neurological Disorders, Immune Disorder, Amputation, Diabetes, Mood Disorders, Skin Condition

💡 Reminder

Don’t panic — you won’t be asked to pay anything or give any personal information. Once you grab the information, you are fully qualified for this Govt Money. After these questions, they will provide you with a Sign-In Form to make your application approved. So sign that form and send it back, okay? And make sure to accept all information about SSDI.

📍 Final Verifications & Warning

Now, can you please verify your first and last name?
Can you verify your zip code?

⚠️ Important Warning

Now, listen very carefully: They are from a different department. They will ask tricky questions to try to disqualify you.

Q6: Did anyone offer you any kind of benefits, money, subsidies, incentives, grants, or a check to talk with us today?

What will your smart answer be — Yes or No?

➡️ Customer Answer:
  • If they say "No" — ✅ Great! Go ahead to the next part.
  • If they say "Yes" — ❗ Repeat the instruction one time: “Please remember — this is a government call, and they don’t allow any kind of money, gifts, or benefits to be offered in exchange for answering. So your answer should be: ‘No, no one offered me anything to talk with you.’ Otherwise, they will disqualify you.”

✅ Final Answer: “No.”

Q7: Did anyone coach you on how to talk with me or answer questions?

✅ Answer: “No one did that.”

Q8: Do you have 30 to 40 minutes to speak with the advisor, right?

✅ “Yes.”

(⚠️ If No – don’t transfer.)

❗ Final Reminders

  • ❌ Do NOT hang up or disconnect during the call.
  • ❌ Do NOT make any mistakes in front of the advisor.
